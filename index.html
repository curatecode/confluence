Great things happen when great minds come together. By partnering, learning or investing on each other, entrepreneurs can turn ideas into business.

What Can We Offer

Space
Physical spaces are channels for people to come together. Its design is key to engineer connections. We handpick space partners who care about serendipity as much as we do: we aim to fill up each square feet with purpose by building vibrant communities in it.
Growth
We aim to grow the number of nodes in the network by increasing interactions - and the probability of successful collisions. We invent good excuses for people to come together: from the geekiest meetups to serendipitous dinners - all we do is to engineer serendipity.
Community
We provide platforms for great entrepreneurs and seasoned professionals to give back, helping high potential founders to emerge. The more successful founders, the biggest the chance that new ones emerge out of their investments, mentoring and (ex)hires.

Our audacious goal is to engineer serendipity: helping founders to find and be found by people who can drive their growth. It's no rocket science: with more interaction, it's more likely that a successful collision happens.
